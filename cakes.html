Carrot Cake

  • 2 Cups Flour
  • 2 Cups Sugar
  • 2 tsp. Baking Soda
  • 2 tsp. Cinnamon
  • 1 tsp. Salt
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 Cup Crisco Oil
  • 3 Cups Finely Grated Carrot
  • Optional: Walnuts

Icing

  • 1 8 oz. Package of Cream Cheese
  • 1 Box Powdered Sugar
  • 1/2 Cube Butter
  • 1 Small Box of Coconut
instructions
  1. Mix all cake ingredients together; Bake at 300° for 30-40 minutes
  2. Mix icing ingredients together, reserving some coconut for the topping

German Chocolate Cake

  • 4 oz. of Sweet Chocolate bar (like Baker's German's)
  • 1/2 Cup Water, boiling
  • 1 Cup Butter
  • 2 Cups Sugar
  • 4 Egg Yolks
  • 4 Egg Whites, stiffly beaten
  • 1 tsp. Vanilla
  • 2 1/4 Cups Sifted All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 tsp. Baking Soda
  • 1/2 tsp. Salt
  • 1 Cup Buttermilk
instructions
  1. Melt chocolate in boiling wtaer, then cool
  2. Cream butter and sugar until fluffy
  3. Add yolks one at a time, beating well after each
  4. Blend in vanilla and chocolate
  5. Sift flour with soda and salt; add alternately with buttermilk to choclate mixture, beating after each addition until smooth
  6. Fold in beaten whites
  7. Pour into three 9-inch layer pans, lined on bottoms with paper
  8. Bake at 350° for 30-35 minutes, cool, frost tops only

Velvety Chocolate Frosting

  • 1/2 Cup Butter
  • 1/4 Cup plus 2 tbs. Milk
  • 4 tbs. Cocoa
  • 1 (16 oz.) Package of Powdered Sugar
instructions
  1. Combine butter, milk, and cocoa in saucepan; bring to boil
  2. Remove from heat; add powdered sugar, stir well
