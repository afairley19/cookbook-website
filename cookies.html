Pecan Cookies

Keith's favorite
  • 1/4 lb. Butter (1 stick, room temp.)
  • 2 Heaping Tbs. Powdered Sugar, plus extra for coating
  • 1 Tsp. Vanilla
  • 1 Tsp. Water
  • 1 Cup Flour
  • 1 Cup Pecans, coursely cut
instructions
  1. Cream butter and measured powdered sugar together
  2. Mix in additional ingredients, "shape like dates"
  3. Bake at 300째 in lower section of oven for 30-45 minutes
  4. When partly cool, shake cookies in a bag of powdered sugar to coat
  5. Note: "eat yum yum as per Jean Fairley"

Pinwheel Cookies

  • 1/2 Cup of Fat (oil)
  • 1/2 Cup Sugar
  • 1 Egg Yolk
  • 1/2 Tsp. Vanilla
  • 3 Tbs. Milk
  • 1 1/2 Tsp. Baking Powder
  • 1/8 Tsp. Salt
  • 1 Sq. Unsweetened Chocolate or 2 tbs. Cocoa
instructions
  1. Use "Cake Method" for mixing
  2. Add melted chocolate to 1/2 of the dough
  3. Roll each into a rectangular sheet
  4. Place sheets together and roll up like a jelly roll
  5. Wrap in wax paper and refrigerate for several hours
  6. Cut into thin slices and bake at 350째 for approx. 10 minutes
