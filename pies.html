Coconut (or Chocolate) Cream Pie

  • Cook & Serve Coconut Jell-O Pudding (or Chocolate)
  • Shaved Coconut
  • 2 Cups of Milk
  • 4 Egg Whites
  • 2 Tbsp. Sugar
instructions
  1. Make Jell-O Pudding according to package instructions (using the milk)
  2. Pour filling into pie crust
  3. Make the meringue topping: Beat 4 egg whites until just thick, then add the 2 tbs. sugar
  4. Beat meringue until peaks form
  5. While filling is still hot, work around outside perimeter of pie and spoon in the meringue (use some force)
  6. Sprinkle top with coconut, bake at 350째 until meringue is browned (don't burn)

Cherry Pie

  • 1 Pkg. Cream Cheese
  • 1 Cup Pwdered Sugar
  • 1 Pkg. Dream Whip
  • 1 Tsp. Vanilla
  • 1 Can Cherry Pie Filling
instructions
  1. Cream together the cream cheese and powdered sugar
  2. Fold Dream Whip and vanilla into mixture and then pour into pie crust
  3. Pour cherry filling on top
  4. Refrigerate for 4 - 6 hours

Pie Crust

  • 3/4 Cup Crisco Shortening
  • 1/4 Cup Boiling Water
  • 2 Cups Flour
instructions
  1. Mix Crisco and water together, cream
  2. Slowly mix in just under 2 cups of the flour
  3. Roll out dough over powdered surface and put into pie dish, puncture bottom of dough with fork
  4. Bake at 350째 for approx. 10 minutes

Pumpkin Pie (Libby's)

  • 3/4 Cup Sugar
  • 1/2 Tsp. Salt
  • 1 Tsp. Ground Cinnamon
  • 1/2 Tsp. Ground Ginger
  • 1/4 Tsp. Ground Cloves
  • 2 large Eggs
  • 1 Can Libby's Pumpkin Pie Filling
  • 1 Can Carnation Evaporated Milk
instructions
  1. Mix together: sugar, cinnamon, ginger, and cloves
  2. Beat eggs in a large bowl, stir in pumpkin and spices, gradually add milk
  3. Pour into pie crust
  4. Bat at 425째 for 15 minutes, reduce temperatire to 350째 and bake an additional 40-50 minutes (or until knife or toothpick comes out clean)
  5. Cool on rack for 2 hours before serving or refrigerating
